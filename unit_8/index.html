At Futbol for Kids it's our goal to deliver as many soccer balls to underprivileged kids as possible. No kid should be denied the chance to kick a ball about. It's one of life's most simple pleasures.

WHERE WE'VE BEEN

Copan is a village located ~3 hours outside of the city central of Honduras. This is where we do most of our work out of as the community is incredibly impoverished and relies heavily on the tourist economy.
