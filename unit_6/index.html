Futbol for Kids

At Futbol for Kids it's our goal to deliver as many soccer balls to underprivileged kids as possible. No kid should be denied the chance to kick a ball about. It's one of life's most simple pleasures. For distribution of the soccer balls Futbol for Kids will both visit communities to deliver soccer balls directly but to also work with travel organizations and mission groups to create a courier network that will allow soccer balls to be delivered worldwide.
